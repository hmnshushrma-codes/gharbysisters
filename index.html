Our Story

We believe homes should feel calm, warm, and personal.

Decodrama was born from a love of creating beautiful, intentional spaces. In a world that moves too fast, we help you slow down — transforming everyday rooms into sanctuaries that reflect your personality and bring you joy.

We handpick every piece, every texture, every detail. From boho living rooms with layered rugs and macramé to cozy bedrooms wrapped in fairy lights — we create spaces that feel like a warm hug.
